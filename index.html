Official Rules

Double Feature Night

Movie Selection
  • Each player secretly picks one B-movie (the cheesier the better).
  • Keep your choice a mystery until it's time to press play.
  • What counts as a B-movie?
    • Low budget
    • Questionable acting
    • Plot holes you could drive a Jeep through
Viewing Protocol
  • Both movies must be watched fully - no skipping scenes, no mercy.
  • Talking is encouraged: mock bad dialogue, spot cheap effects!
The B-Movie Index (1-10 each)

Rate from 1 (normal movie) to 10 (glorious disaster)

  1. 🎭 Overacting - How exaggerated are the performances?
  2. 💥 Action & Explosions - How ridiculous is the action?
  3. 🩸 Practical Effects & Gore - How fake and messy?
  4. 🖥️ CGI & Visual Crimes - How bad is the digital stuff?
  5. 🤯 Plot Confusion - How hard is it to follow?
  6. 👹 Creature / Monster - How fake does the monster look?
  7. 💬 Dialogue Disaster - How bad are the lines?

Max Score = 70 points (7 categories × 10)

🎉 Bonus: Enjoyment
  • How much fun did you have? (1-10)
  • Tracked separately - doesn't count toward total.
