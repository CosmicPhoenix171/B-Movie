Official Rules

Double Feature Night

Movie Selection
  • Each player secretly picks one B-movie (the cheesier the better).
  • Keep your choice a mystery until it's time to press play.
  • What counts as a B-movie?
    • Low budget
    • Questionable acting
    • Plot holes you could drive a Jeep through
Viewing Protocol
  • Both movies must be watched fully - no skipping scenes, no mercy.
  • Talking is encouraged: mock bad dialogue, spot cheap effects!
The Good-Bad Movie Index (-5 to 5 each)

Rate from -5 (masterpiece side) to 5 (cheesiest possible). 0 = none.

  1. 🎭 Overacting - How exaggerated were the performances?
  2. 💥 Explosions & Destruction - How fun was the chaos?
  3. 🎬 Action - How entertaining were fights/chases/stunts?
  4. 🧟 Practical Effects - How fun were props and makeup?
  5. 🩸 Gore - How entertaining was the blood?
  6. 🧬 CGI Crimes - How funny was the digital work?
  7. 🧠 Plot Chaos - How entertaining was the nonsense?
  8. 🦖 Creature - How fun was the monster?
  9. 🗣 Dialogue - How quotable was the writing?
  10. ❤️ Enjoyment - How much fun did you have?

Max Points = 50 using absolute values. Cheese Score runs from -50 to 50.
